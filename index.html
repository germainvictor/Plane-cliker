RULES OF THE GAME

Finally, you did it ! You created your own airline. Now, you, CEO of Air Clicker, have to carry your company in order to get it from zero to international market leader ! So buy new planes for increasing your tickets prices, hire the greatest salesmans who will sale tickets for you, and sign juicy contracts with big sponsors in order to gain easy money. Go on, it’s up to you, and become the greatest airline in the world !

Primary goal : Sell as much tickets as you can in order to buy better and better upgrades and reach the top of the airlines market. The most important : when you click, you sell a ticket and earn money. Simple.

1. Money counter - Let you know how much money you have
2. Valuation counter - Total of the money you got and money you spent
3. Boarding pass - Click it to sell tickets ! At the beginning, 1 ticket = 1£
4. Home - Get you back to the arrival screen
5. Help - Get you to the user manual
6. Upgrades screen - Here you can buy planes, sponsors, and salesman.
7. Planes counter - Show you how much planes of each sorts you have
8. Social networks - you can share your progress in the game !

Plane - More planes will increase your capacity carry passengers, so to sell tickets and increase your clicking power
Salesman - Hire salesman in your company : they will go and sell tons of tickets without you knowing it ! (auto-click : generate money automatically )
Sponsors - Sponsors are ready to pay well in order to stick theirs logo on your planes :with this, you selling capacity will increase a lot for a short period. (Temporary power-ups who boost your clicking power)
